# Chronoflow
chronoflow is a powerful and flexible terminal-based time tracking and productivity tool designed to help users manage their tasks and time efficiently. It offers a range of features including task management, time tracking, reporting, and integration with other tools.

## Features
- **Task Management**: Create, edit, and organize tasks with ease. Assign priorities, deadlines, and tags to keep track of your work.
- **Daily Planning**: Plan your day by scheduling tasks and setting reminders to stay on top of your responsibilities.
